Meet Tommy A Front-End Web Developer.

About Me:

Hi, Im Tommy an aspiring Front-End developer and designer. I spent the first Part of my adult life trying to out my place in this world like a lot of young adults. I drifted between a few jobs in the hospitality field, a fun and challenging field where I found a passion for customer service and product knowledge. After years of managing restaurants with the help my older brother I started to attend university, there I gravitated toward social sciences specifically psychology. After some Time it was clear to me that I wasn’t going to be happy in that field and started to look for what really made me happy. Later in life later than I would care to admit through long conversations with family and friends I discovered a true passion for creating and building with computer languages. After this discovery I ran head first into everything I could absorb about the field, talking with friends in the space already, learning online through free videos and practicing these skill in my free time all while continuing my education in university. As of now I am enrolled in Treehouses’s front-end developer boot camp with my eyes on full stack development. I am a graduate of Moshfegh Hamedani front end boot camp who’s lectures and programs I still go back to, to brush up on my skills as needed. I have along way to go but I am a student. I will forever be a student of this field that I have fallen in love with. I will never stop learning because I am truly passionate about being the best developer I can be. I truly enjoy every success and failure that I make because I have learned something new to advance my skills.
